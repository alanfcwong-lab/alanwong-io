Methodology

How this assessment works and where the framework comes from

This assessment uses the Sidekick 4-Stage AI Adoption Framework (Awareness → Experimentation → Operationalization → Transformation) to evaluate organizational readiness across 6 dimensions: Leadership & Strategy, Data & Infrastructure, Team Capability, Process Maturity, Culture & Change Readiness, and Governance & Ethics. Each of the 18 questions maps to a 1–4 maturity score. Dimension averages produce the radar profile; the total score (normalized to 100) determines the overall stage.

The framework is informed by industry-standard AI maturity models and real-world adoption patterns observed across mid-market organizations. Recommendations are tailored to each stage with actionable 90-day priorities.

References: Sidekick AI Adoption Framework · McKinsey State of AI · Stanford HAI AI Index · Gartner AI Maturity
